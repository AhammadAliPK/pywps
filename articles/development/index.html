Join the development!

All new developers are welcome. To join the development team, with write access to svn, you have to perform following steps:

  1. Register yourself at http://wald.intevation.org
  2. Request to join PyWPS development.
  3. After you will be accepted, you have to edit your ssh public key
  4. Download the fresh svn version as new PyWPS developer: 
    svn checkout svn+ssh://developername@svn.wald.intevation.org/pywps/trunk
